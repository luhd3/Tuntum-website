About Manipur

Manipur, a beautiful and culturally rich state in northeast India, is famous for Loktak Lake, Kangla Fort, Shirui Hill, vibrant dance forms, handloom crafts, and traditional foods like Eromba and Chakhao.

Culture & Cuisine

Manipur is home to traditional dances like Ras Leela, vibrant handloom textiles, and delicious food such as Eromba (vegetable mash) and Chakhao (black rice dessert). The state is a mosaic of over 30 tribes, each with unique traditions.
