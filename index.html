Biography

Full Name: Naruto Uzumaki

Age: Born October 10, Naruto is in his late 30s in Boruto series.

Status: Seventh Hokage of Konohagakure

Surname Meaning: "Uzumaki" means spiral or whirlpool, a symbol of his clan.

Backstory: Naruto was orphaned at birth after the Nine-Tails attacked Konoha. Despite growing up lonely, he pursued his dream of becoming Hokage and protecting his village.

Family

  • Father: Minato Namikaze (Fourth Hokage)
  • Mother: Kushina Uzumaki
  • Wife: Hinata Hyuga
  • Son: Boruto Uzumaki
  • Daughter: Himawari Uzumaki

Achievements

  • Became the Seventh Hokage
  • Mastered Sage Mode
  • Perfected Rasengan and developed Rasenshuriken
  • Built peace among the five great nations
  • Redeemed Sasuke and ended the cycle of hatred

Major Battles

  • Defeated Pain/Nagato
  • Fought Obito Uchiha and Madara Uchiha
  • Sealed Kaguya Ōtsutsuki with Sasuke
  • Final Valley Battle against Sasuke Uchiha
  • Fought Momoshiki Ōtsutsuki alongside Sasuke
